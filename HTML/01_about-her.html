Ruthie Bomochie Armah holds a bachelor degree in Petrochemical Engineering from Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology. She was awarded the KNUST Engineering Education Project (KEEP) scholarship to further her studies in MPhil Chemical Engineering. Presently, she is the best female student in her class.

Moreover, she has passion for the use of data science and Artificial Intelligence to solve problems across the African continent. She is currently a member of the Ghanaian Chapter of ‘Women in Data Science and Machine Learning’ and ‘She Code Africa’ community. She was awarded a Microsoft Cloud and Data Scholarship for a Microsoft certification in Data Analysis and Visualization, amongst many other awards. She has extensive knowledge and experience in the use of data analysis and visualization tools, and the creation of automated systems using open-sourced resources.
Moreover, she has experience and interest in both traditional and agile project management. She has worked as project manager for several impact and evaluation consultancy projects for international development and corporate clients, such as, Swedfund International, MTN Ghana, etc. In her work in this area, efficiency through data science tools features prominently, making her work well- coordinated.

Currently, Ruthie is a member of the Love Economy Church, Alpha Branch where she is active in the music ministry. She speaks two major languages; French and English and loves to spend time reading novels and professional development books.

In the next ten years, she aspires to be one of the leading women in AI in Africa and on the globe. She would also continue with her doctoral studies soon after her Master’s degree.
